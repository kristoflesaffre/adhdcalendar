# Product

## Register

product

## Users

People who rely on calendar alarms for time-sensitive appointments, errands, work blocks, and tasks. They may use the app in a browser or iOS wrapper and need reminders that are hard to miss.

## Product Purpose

ADHD Calendar is a Google Calendar-style app focused on real alarms: events and timed tasks can ring until dismissed, sync with Google Calendar, and survive common calendar workflows without becoming polite background notifications.

## Brand Personality

Practical, direct, reassuring. The product should feel calm enough for daily planning, but serious about alarms doing their job.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-page theatrics, decorative UI that slows down task flow, and vague reminder patterns that hide critical alarm behavior behind generic notification language.

## Design Principles

- Make alarm behavior explicit and easy to trust.
- Keep controls compact, familiar, and fast to scan.
- Preserve the user's flow when creating or editing events and tasks.
- Favor clarity over novelty in core calendar interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable labels, standard controls, clear focus states, and motion that supports state changes rather than decoration. Alarm actions should remain usable across desktop, mobile, and iOS wrapper contexts.
